What We Do

At Mindful Leaps Therapy, we specialise in paediatric occupational therapy services tailored to support the unique needs of children and their families. Our holistic, neuro-affirming approach empowers each child to reach their fullest potential. Here’s an overview of the services we provide:

Idividualized Assessments


We conduct comprehensive assessments to identify each child's strengths, challenges, and sensory processing patterns, allowing us to create personalised therapy plans that address their specific needs.



Goal-Oriented Interventions


Our team develops individualised, measurable goals focused on enhancing daily functioning, independence, and overall quality of life. Our interventions are engaging and aligned with the child's interests.

Sensory Processing and Modulation


We provide support in sensory processing and modulation to help children effectively respond to sensory information. Tailored activities promote sensory regulation and enhance attention and focus.



Fine and Gross Motor Skill Development


We implement activities and exercises aimed at improving fine motor skills (such as handwriting and self-care tasks) and gross motor skills (such as coordination and balance), enabling children to participate fully in daily activities.

Self-Care Skills Development


We teach and reinforce essential self-care skills, including dressing, grooming, and hygiene routines, to promote independence and confidence in personal care.



Social Skills and Emotional Regulation


We support children in developing social skills and emotional regulation strategies, helping them navigate interactions with peers and manage their emotions in various settings.

Family-Centred Support


We actively involve families in the therapy process, providing education, resources, and strategies that can be implemented at home and in the community to reinforce progress.



School Consultations and Collaborations


We collaborate with educators and school staff to ensure children receive the necessary supports within the educational environment. This includes sensory accommodations, classroom modifications, and staff training.

Workshops and Community Education


We offer workshops and community education sessions to raise awareness about paediatric occupational therapy, neurodiversity, and strategies for supporting children’s development across various contexts.



Play-Based Therapy


Play is a fundamental component of our therapy sessions. We recognise that play is essential for learning and development, allowing children to practice new skills, express themselves, and build confidence.

The Therapy Process

At Mindful Leaps Therapy, our therapy process is structured in clear stages to ensure a comprehensive and effective approach to each child’s development. Here’s how we guide our clients through their therapeutic journey:

Initial Consultation

We begin with an initial consultation to understand the child’s background, concerns, and specific needs. This stage involves discussing family goals and expectations to ensure a collaborative approach.

Comprehensive Assessment

A thorough assessment is conducted to evaluate the child’s strengths, challenges, and sensory processing patterns. This assessment may include standardised tests, observations, and parent/caregiver input, providing a holistic view of the child’s abilities.

Individualized Goal Setting

Based on the assessment results, we work with the child and their family to establish individualised, measurable goals. These goals focus on enhancing the child’s daily functioning, independence, and overall quality of life.

Personalized Therapy Plan Development

We create a tailored therapy plan that outlines the specific interventions and strategies to be implemented. This plan is designed to be engaging and relevant to the child’s interests, ensuring a motivating therapy experience.

Therapy Sessions

During therapy sessions, we implement the personalised interventions. Our approach incorporates play-based activities, sensory processing techniques, skill development exercises, and emotional regulation strategies, all in a supportive environment.

Family Involvement and Education

We actively involve parents and caregivers throughout the therapy process. We provide education, resources, and strategies to implement at home, empowering families to support their child's progress beyond the therapy sessions.

Progress Monitoring and Adjustments

We regularly monitor the child’s progress towards their goals, using observations and feedback from parents and teachers. Adjustments to the therapy plan are made as needed to ensure that it remains effective and aligned with the child’s evolving needs.

Collaboration with Schools and Community

We collaborate with educators and community members to ensure that the child receives the necessary support in their everyday environments. This may involve consultations, training, and recommendations for accommodations.

Ongoing Evaluation

Continuous evaluation of the child’s progress allows us to celebrate successes and identify new areas for growth. We schedule periodic reviews to assess the effectiveness of the therapy plan and to set new goals as appropriate.

Transition Planning

As the child progresses and achieves their goals, we work with families to create a transition plan that supports the continuation of skills and strategies learned during therapy. This ensures a smooth transition to independence and confidence in everyday activities.
