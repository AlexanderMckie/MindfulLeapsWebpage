About Us

At Mindful Leaps Therapy, we offer mobile Occupational Therapy services for children and adolescents up to 18 years of age across Melbourne's northern suburbs. Our therapy is grounded in evidence-based practice, focusing on the unique goals and developmental needs of each child. Sessions are designed to be fun, creative, and can take place in homes, day-cares, kindergartens, or schools.

We are committed to a client-centered and family-centered approach, ensuring that parents and caregivers are actively involved in the therapy process. Our Mission

At Mindful Leaps Therapy, our mission is to help children and their families reach their fullest potential through engaging and evidence-based paediatric occupational therapy. We create supportive environments that promote emotional well-being, sensory regulation, and skill development. By fostering confidence and resilience through playful, client-centered care, we aim to make meaningful leaps towards independence, inclusion, and an improved quality of life for every family we serve.
